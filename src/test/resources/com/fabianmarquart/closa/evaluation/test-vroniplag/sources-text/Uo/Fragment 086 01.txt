[Seite 38] Censorship made a greater difference in the production of newsreels. Newsreel- makers, for example, could not show harsh conditions of labour.11 Censors forbade [Seite 39] the filming of strikes and other anti-government disturbances in 1905. As a result, the historical record that is available today in Russian film archives is spotty. We have pictures of the Tsar meeting foreign dignitaries, but almost nothing on the crucial events of the First Russian Revolution. 11 Khanzhonkov, p.76.
