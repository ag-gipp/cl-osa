The increase in the SDR shows little sign of slowing down, and the differences from rates in other reference countries and from that in the EU have continued to increase because of the declining trend in these countries. The rate in Hungary in 1998 was more than twice the EU average in 1996 (Fig. 16, 17). [Seite 15, Zeile 3 ff.] In recent years, the SDR has not increased further, although it is still 55% higher than the EU average, which has been declining constantly since 1970 (Fig. 18, 19).
